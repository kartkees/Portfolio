Tony's Chocolonely


At Tony’s Chocolonely I was the Wrapper Delight (aka junior creative). I was the second designer in the design team. So what did I do? Just think of anything visual from Tony’s. From wrapper, social post to more elaborate outings like the brand store and the boat for Plastic Whale. At Tony’s I got to combine my skills as graphic designer and bad pun teller. You’re constantly asked to be critical and think outside the box, two values which I hold dearly with everything I do.



Mothersday

This was one of the very first projects I got to do all by myself. Every mothers day a special bar is made to gift to all mothers.



Plastic Whale

In August of 2017 Plastic Whale and Tony’s decided to team up. Added to Plastic Whales’s fleet would be a Tony’s boat. And since Tony’s always wants to do something out of the box we didn’t just give them a few design elements to copy paste, we wanted to create our own crazy design. On the outside of the boast I put bars that when in the water seem like swimming. All saying something nautical, flavour or Tony’s themed. On the inside of the boat we have two important design elements. The first is our manifesto so everyone on the boat can learn more about Tony’s cause while plastic fishing. Second we replaced the standard Plastic Whale flooring of all recycled bottle caps by a floor made of cacao beans and bottle caps in the Tony colours. Creating a beautiful mosaic of both company’s core raw material.
